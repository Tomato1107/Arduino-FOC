Where the <i>u(k)</i> is the control signal (voltage <i>U<sub>q</sub></i> in our case) in moment <i>k</i>, <i>e(k),e(k-1)</i> is the tracking error in current moment <i>k</i> and previous step <i>k-1</i>.
